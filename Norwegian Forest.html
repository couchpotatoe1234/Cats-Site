Norwegian Forest

The Norwegian forest, sometimes called "Wegie" is a Longhair with a fun personality. It has a lot of thick, dense fur all over its neck, chest, and paws. They are very independent and intelligent, but can also be fun at times, just randomly getting a burst of energy to chase after some toy. They may make chirping and purring sounds when they are excited. They mature rather slowly, fully maturing at around age 5 where they get pretty large.
